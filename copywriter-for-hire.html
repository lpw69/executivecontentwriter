How to Hire the Right Copywriter

Essential guidance for making the right copywriting hire

1. Define Your Objectives

Before hiring a copywriter, clarify what you want to achieve. Are you building thought leadership, generating leads, supporting sales, or launching products? Clear objectives help you find a copywriter with relevant expertise and evaluate their proposals effectively.

2. Look for Specialized Experience

Generic copywriters may struggle with your industry's unique challenges. Look for writers with relevant sector experience - technology, finance, healthcare, or B2B services. Specialized knowledge translates into copy that resonates with your audience and demonstrates credibility.

3. Evaluate Their Process

Professional copywriters have systematic approaches to understanding your business, audience, and voice. Ask about their discovery process, how they learn your industry, and their method for capturing your communication style. A structured process indicates professional competence.

4. Review Actual Results

Don't just look at writing samples - ask about business outcomes. Can they show increased engagement, lead generation, or sales results? Professional copywriters track performance and can demonstrate how their copy drives business results, not just creates content.

5. Understand Their Expertise Range

The best copywriters can handle diverse content types - from LinkedIn posts and email sequences to video sales letters and Facebook ads. Versatility indicates deep copywriting knowledge rather than narrow specialization in one format.

What to Look for in a Professional Copywriter

Strategic Thinking

Look for copywriters who ask about your business objectives, target audience, and competitive landscape. They should understand how copy fits into your broader marketing and business strategy, not just create isolated content pieces.

Conversion Focus

Professional copywriters understand that the goal isn't just engagement - it's driving action. They should discuss conversion principles, psychology of persuasion, and how to move prospects through your sales process effectively.

Voice Capture Ability

Exceptional copywriters can write in your authentic voice while maintaining professional standards. They should have a process for learning your communication style and ensuring consistency across all content.

Multi-Format Expertise

The best copywriters can adapt their skills across formats - LinkedIn content, email marketing, sales pages, video scripts, and advertising copy. This versatility demonstrates deep copywriting knowledge rather than format-specific skills.

Results Measurement

Professional copywriters track and optimize performance. They should discuss how they measure success, what metrics matter for your objectives, and how they continuously improve results over time.

Industry Credibility

Look for copywriters with relevant industry experience or background. Understanding your sector's challenges, terminology, and audience behavior significantly improves copy effectiveness and authenticity.

Copywriter Hiring Checklist

Essential questions and considerations before making your decision

Experience & Expertise

  • Do they have relevant industry experience?
  • Can they show measurable results from previous work?
  • Do they understand your target audience and market?
  • Have they worked with similar business models or company stages?
  • Can they write across multiple formats and channels?

Process & Approach

  • Do they have a structured onboarding and discovery process?
  • How do they learn your voice and communication style?
  • What's their content planning and strategy development approach?
  • How do they handle revisions and feedback?
  • Do they provide performance tracking and optimization?

Working Relationship

  • Will you work directly with the copywriter or through account managers?
  • What are their communication preferences and response times?
  • How do they handle rush projects or urgent requests?
  • Are they available for strategy calls and consultations?
  • Do they offer flexibility in scope and deliverables?

Investment & Value

  • Is their pricing transparent and aligned with your budget?
  • What's included in their standard packages?
  • Do they focus on deliverables or business outcomes?
  • Are there additional costs for strategy, revisions, or consultation?
  • Can they demonstrate ROI from their copywriting services?
