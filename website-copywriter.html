What Does a Website Copywriter Do?

Understanding the specialized role of website copywriters in driving conversions and business growth through strategic web copy

A website copywriter creates persuasive copy for websites designed to convert visitors into customers, leads, or subscribers. Unlike content writers who focus on informational content, website copywriters specialize in conversion-focused copy that drives specific business actions and measurable results.

Professional website copywriters understand user psychology, conversion optimization, and the strategic elements that transform website visitors into paying customers. They create copy that not only engages readers but guides them through a carefully crafted journey toward taking desired actions.

Types of Website Copy Professional Copywriters Create

Website copywriters handle multiple types of web copy, each serving specific conversion goals:

Homepage Copy

The most critical page on any website, homepage copy must immediately communicate value proposition, build credibility, and guide visitors toward conversion actions. Professional website copywriters craft homepage copy that converts first-time visitors into engaged prospects.

Landing Page Copy

Specialized pages designed for specific campaigns or offers, landing pages require focused copy that eliminates distractions and drives single conversion actions. Website copywriters optimize landing page copy for maximum conversion rates.

About Page Copy

Often the second-most visited page, About pages build trust and credibility while positioning the company or executive as the ideal solution. Professional copywriters create About page copy that converts skeptics into believers.

Service and Product Pages

These pages must balance detailed information with persuasive elements that overcome objections and drive purchase decisions. Website copywriters craft service pages that educate while they sell.

B2B Website Copywriting Expertise

Specialized website copywriting for business-to-business companies and professional service providers

Why B2B Website Copy Requires Specialization

B2B website copywriting differs significantly from consumer-focused copy. Business buyers have longer decision cycles, multiple stakeholders, and require detailed information while maintaining professional credibility throughout the evaluation process.

Understanding B2B Buyer Psychology

B2B website copywriters must understand how business decision-makers evaluate solutions, what information they need at each stage of the buying process, and how to address both logical and emotional factors that influence business purchases.

Technical Credibility and Professional Positioning

B2B buyers expect website copy that demonstrates industry expertise, technical competence, and professional credibility. Generic website copywriters often lack the business background needed to create convincing B2B copy.

Stakeholder Communication

B2B purchases often involve multiple decision-makers. Professional B2B website copywriters create copy that speaks to different stakeholder concerns while maintaining coherent messaging throughout the website.

B2B Website Copy That Converts

Effective B2B website copywriting balances detailed information with persuasive elements that guide prospects through complex decision-making processes:

  • Value Proposition Clarity: Immediate communication of business benefits and competitive advantages
  • Trust Building Elements: Credibility indicators, case studies, and social proof that reduce perceived risk
  • Technical Accuracy: Industry-specific terminology and concepts that demonstrate expertise
  • Decision Support: Information architecture that supports complex evaluation processes
  • Professional Tone: Executive-level communication that resonates with business decision-makers

Website Copywriter FAQ

Common questions about hiring professional website copywriters and web copywriting services

What does a website copywriter do?

A website copywriter creates persuasive copy for websites including homepage copy, landing pages, about pages, service descriptions, and product pages that convert visitors into customers. They focus on conversion optimization rather than just informational content.

How much does a professional website copywriter cost?

Professional website copywriters typically charge $2,500-$6,500 for complete website copywriting projects, or $150-$500 per page depending on complexity and research required. Investment varies based on website size and conversion goals.

What's the difference between website copywriting and content writing?

Website copywriting focuses on persuasive copy that drives conversions and sales, while content writing creates informational content like blog posts and articles for education and engagement. Website copywriters optimize for business results.

How long does website copywriting take?

Professional website copywriting projects typically take 2-6 weeks depending on scope. This includes research, strategy development, copy creation, and revisions. Larger websites or complex businesses may require additional time.

Do website copywriters help with SEO?

Yes, professional website copywriters understand SEO basics and create copy that balances search optimization with conversion goals. However, they focus primarily on user experience and conversion rather than pure SEO optimization.

What makes B2B website copywriting different?

B2B website copywriting requires understanding business buyer psychology, longer sales cycles, multiple stakeholders, and professional communication standards. It focuses on building credibility and trust with business decision-makers.

How do website copywriters improve conversion rates?

Website copywriters improve conversions through clear value propositions, addressing buyer objections, optimizing calls-to-action, creating logical information flow, and using persuasive elements that guide visitors toward desired actions.

Should I hire a website copywriter or use AI?

Professional website copywriters provide strategic thinking, industry expertise, and conversion optimization knowledge that AI cannot replicate. They understand business psychology and create copy that reflects authentic brand voice and professional credibility.
