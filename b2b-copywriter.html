What Does a B2B Copywriter Do?

A B2B copywriter creates strategic copy and content specifically for business-to-business companies, focusing on complex sales cycles, multiple decision makers, and technical products or services. Unlike B2C copywriters, professional B2B copywriters understand enterprise buyer behavior, industry terminology, and the nuanced messaging required to influence business purchase decisions.

As your dedicated business copywriter, I combine 10 years of corporate consulting and sales experience with world-class copywriting principles from legends like Eugene Schwartz and David Ogilvy. Frequently Asked Questions About B2B Copywriting

What's the difference between hiring a freelance B2B copywriter vs an agency?

Freelance B2B copywriters offer direct access, specialized expertise, and typically lower costs ($2,500-$5,000/month vs $8,000-$15,000 for agencies). You work directly with the person writing your content, ensuring consistency and deep understanding of your business. Agencies provide broader services but often use junior writers while charging premium rates.

How long does it take to see results from B2B copywriting?

LinkedIn content and executive communications typically show engagement improvements within 2-4 weeks. Sales copy and email sequences may take 30-60 days to optimize based on performance data. Long-term thought leadership and brand building develop over 3-6 months of consistent, strategic content creation.

Do you write copy for technical B2B companies?

Yes, with 10 years of corporate consulting experience and a cybersecurity background, I specialize in technical B2B companies. This includes SaaS platforms, cybersecurity solutions, fintech applications, and enterprise software where technical credibility is essential for effective messaging.

What makes B2B copywriting different from regular business writing?

B2B copywriting combines strategic messaging with proven conversion principles. Unlike general business writing, it's designed to influence purchase decisions, build executive authority, and support complex sales processes. It requires understanding both copywriting psychology and enterprise buyer behavior.

Can you help with LinkedIn content for executives?

Executive LinkedIn content is one of my core specialties. I work with startup founders and corporate executives to create authentic thought leadership content that builds industry authority, attracts business opportunities, and supports personal brand development. This includes posts, articles, and strategic commenting.

Do you work with international B2B companies?

Yes, I work with B2B companies globally, with particular experience serving clients in the UK, Netherlands, United States, and Canada. Understanding international business contexts and communication styles is part of effective B2B copywriting for global companies.
