How to Find the Best SEO Content Writer for Your Business

When businesses search "how to find SEO content writer" or "what to look for in SEO content writer," they need a clear framework for making the right hiring decision. Here's what actually matters:

Industry-Specific Experience Over General Writing

Red Flag: "I write for all industries" or portfolios with random topics

Green Flag: Deep expertise in your specific sector (B2B SaaS, fintech, healthcare, etc.) with proven results

Why It Matters: B2B buyers expect industry credibility. Generic content gets ignored; specialized content gets shared and drives conversions.

Business Results Over Traffic Numbers

Red Flag: "We increased traffic by 400%" without conversion context

Green Flag: "Generated 156 qualified leads and $2.3M pipeline from content strategy"

Why It Matters: Traffic is vanity; revenue is sanity. You need content that drives business outcomes.
